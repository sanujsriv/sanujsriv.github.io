About Research

  • My research interest is mainly focused on aspects of text-analytics better known as Natural Language Understanding(NLU).
  • Specifically speaking, I have worked on topic modeling & topic semantic visualization where we model unstructured text bodies to infer its hidden semantic structure, as topics, in a unsupervised fashion
  • Throughout my research, I became familiar with a variety of concepts - Multilabel classification, Variational Inference, Clustering, Maximum Likelihood Estimation ,Maximum A posterior, Neural Networks, etc.
  • For my future research, I intend to work on graph mining and graph networks. I am also interested in working on Variational Autoencoders
  • Like everyone, I explore to learn new things and polish my growth as a person. I would love to contribute more to everything that has helped me get here.
